About us

We care about Reggis.

Our drive: Allowing coexistance with all reggis.

Founded in 2017, Reggi.com has become the go to website to learn about the creatures called "Reggis". We serve as a non profit organization dedicated to educating the public on these goofballs. we hope to create a world where reggies and humans can exist peacefully.

Our efforts have resulted in a growing public acceptance of domesticated reggis, and a better understanding of wild reggis. After only a year of the site coming online, reggi hunting rates dropped 36%, and reggi adoption rates have risen 17%. We won't stop until we create the perfect world for reggis to inhabit.
